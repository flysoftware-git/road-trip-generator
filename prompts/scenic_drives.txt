Destination:  {destination_name}

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT QUALIFIES AS AN ENTRY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The label "scenic drive" is a UI convention. Valid entries include any of:

  1. Named scenic drives or byways (paved or unpaved)
  2. Significant viewpoints reached by a short drive from the destination
     (rim overlooks, summit pull-outs, canyon viewpoints)
  3. Aerial or gondola experiences providing a scenic overview of the area
  4. Day-trip drives originating from the destination (mountain passes,
     canyon loops, historic highway segments)
  5. Historic roads with distinctive character worth a dedicated entry

Exclude: hiking trails (those belong in top_attractions), routine park
  entrance roads that are simply the way in with no stop value of their own.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
QUANTITY AND QUALITY RULES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- Include 2–4 entries per destination
- Quality over quantity — 2 richly detailed entries beat 4 thin ones
- For destinations with a well-known named drive (Zion Canyon Scenic Drive,
  Rim Drive, Utah Scenic Byway 12), that drive is always the first entry
- For town-based or urban destinations (Telluride, Santa Fe, Moab):
  include at least one day_trip drive radiating from the destination
- For gondola/aerial entries: describe the aerial perspective itself —
  what terrain it crosses, what summits or canyons appear below, what
  the view looks like from the top, and any operating schedule notes
- Viewpoint entries: give the driving distance from the nearest town,
  describe the specific vista (compass direction, named peaks or formations
  visible, scale), and note the parking situation

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STRICT PROHIBITIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- NEVER include URLs or web addresses
- NEVER invent road names, pass names, or viewpoint names
- title field becomes a JS key — it must exactly match what will appear
  on the popup button in the HTML output; keep it stable and precise
- Do not use: stunning, breathtaking, iconic, spectacular, majestic,
  charming, nestled, world-class
